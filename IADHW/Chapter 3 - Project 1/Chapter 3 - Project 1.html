Qingce Village

Details

The first photo is Qingce Village.
Located in the far north of Liyue, the village is hidden among the hills and bamboo forests. Rows of half-moon terraced fields dot the cloud-shrouded valley, and the mountain breeze reveals layers of colorful waves. In this place like a paradise, the villagers of Qingce Village live a simple and full life

Qinfyun Peak

Details

The second photo is Qingyun Peak.
Glaze on the northwest of the year clouds and fog, abrupt mountains without people. The mountain has a strange scenery, but because of the complex terrain, steep mountain path and few people, it is rumored to be a reclusive place for the fairies who guard the moon of glaze. Therefore, it is also sought after by some people who pray for gods and blessings. Take fairy jump wall must go to the place, climb the mountain climbing is very tired but as long as climb up can enjoy the feeling of flying freely.

Dihuazhou

Details

The third photo is Dihuazhou.
Dihuazhou is named after the large area of growth in the shoal Dihuazhou is a natural shoal wetland in the north of Liyue. It is a rich ecosystem of diverse animal and plant communities, and a wonderful sight for travelers. At the same time, it was also the only road from Mund to Liyue by land, so caravans often stopped there. Every player on their way to Glaze Moon will pass by here and use the main character to unlock the character's rock attributes.
